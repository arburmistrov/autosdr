THE CHALLENGE

Most banks have AI activity, but not an AI operating model.

Pilots stay disconnected from production because architecture, governance, and business ownership are not linked end-to-end.

Fragmented tooling

Copilot, low-code, and custom stacks run in parallel without clear selection logic per use case.

Weak lifecycle governance

Teams can prototype, but formal review, deployment, monitoring, and versioning remain manual.

Risk of compliance gaps

Identity, content safety, logging, and approval controls are often bolted on after development.

Limited business trust

Without explainability and controlled rollout, leadership cannot confidently scale AI into core flows.

THE VISION

One platform to design, govern, and scale banking AI agents.

A productized foundation combining Azure AI Foundry, reusable tooling microservices, and a business-friendly control layer.

Experience Layer

Agent portal and reviewer UI with role-based access for business and risk stakeholders.

Orchestration Layer

Workflow engine for multi-step agent execution, tool routing, and human-in-the-loop controls.

Tooling Layer

Reusable services: deep research, provider connectors, PII pseudonymization, and report composer.

Knowledge Layer

OCR and ingestion, blob storage, vector + keyword search, and runstate tracking for retrieval quality.

Control Layer

Entra ID RBAC, Key Vault, safety filters, audit telemetry, and model/agent version management.

        Azure AI Foundry

        Best for advanced, high-control, custom-tooling use cases with strict governance requirements.
